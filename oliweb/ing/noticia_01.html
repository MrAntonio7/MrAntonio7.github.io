The price of oil goes up

The price of oil rose again last week

The price of oil rose slightly last week in all categories, an increase that was also accompanied by an increase in sales. This ends with the moderate downward trend that had taken place at the beginning of the olive harvesting campaign and consolidates the price stability that began to become noticeable last week.

Thus, from November 24 to 30 the average price at which the kilo of oil was sold at source was 3,521 euros. In the case of the extra virgin, the 3,577 euros were reached; in the virgin to 3,454 and in the lampante to 3,338 euros per kilo in origin.
