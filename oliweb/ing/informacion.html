That's how we are at OLIWEB!

OLIWEB is born, and, from the first moment, we bet on the online channel as our main business focus. Something that in those years was still difficult to assimilate, soon became our main hallmark.

Maintaining the character of the small business, the closeness to the client, the personalized treatment are the premises on which our philosophy revolves. These maxims boosted us to become a benchmark for online sales.

At this point, we can only thank the trust placed in us and the effort of all the people who have passed through here to make OLIWEB, be it and continue to be, your trusted store.
